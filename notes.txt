FORMATS FOR FILES

Student format: 3 lines
<username> <password> <nickname>
<classes>
<coursesTaken>

Admin format: 1 line
<username> <password> <nickname>

Classes format: 2 lines
<classCode> <classCourse> <nUnits> <class_name> <class_Prof> <classSection> <classDay> <classStart> <classEnd> <classRoom> <classCap> <classNStudents>
<coursePreReqs>
